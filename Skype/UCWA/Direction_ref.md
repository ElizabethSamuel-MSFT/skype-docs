# Direction


_** Applies to: **Skype for Business 2015_

Represents the direction of the call, message, etc.
            
## Members



|Name|Description|
|:-----|:-----|
|Incoming|Represents the incoming direction.|
|Outgoing|Represents the outgoing direction.|
